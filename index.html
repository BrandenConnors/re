About Branden
CA DRE #02229056

A Napa native, Branden was born and raised in the valley. He offers in-depth expertise into each of Napa's unique neighborhoods, as well as a broad knowledge of the Yountville and St Helena real estate markets.

Branden is a versatile and results-driven professional in the luxury real estate sector. He excels in preparing and marketing high-value residential properties and is skilled at representing clients across various purchasing tiers. Dedicated to providing top-notch service, Branden ensures seamless processes and operations, focusing on attentive listening, meticulous organization, and up-to-date knowledge of housing regulations.

Clients praise Branden for his unwavering dedication, prompt responsiveness, tireless diligence, patient demeanor, meticulous attention to detail, transparent honesty, amiable nature, and efficient workflow. Renowned for his negotiation skills and discretion, Branden offers refined market insights tailored to his clients' needs, fostering strong and lasting relationships.
